## What I collect

When you contact Dr. Balsara by phone or email, I record the information you choose to share — typically your name, contact details, your pet's name and history, and your address for the home visit.

## How I use it

I use this information solely to provide veterinary end-of-life services and to follow up with you about your visit. I do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

## Aftercare partners

If you choose private or communal cremation, I share only the information necessary (your pet's name, weight, and your contact details) with the aftercare provider you select.

## Records

Veterinary records are retained as required by the [College of Veterinarians of British Columbia](https://www.cvbc.ca/) and applicable BC privacy law (PIPA).
