## In-Home Euthanasia

A peaceful, unhurried visit in the comfort of your home. The visit typically lasts 60 minutes and is paced to your family, not to a clock.

Includes:

- A brief consultation about your pet's history and current quality of life
- A gentle sedative so your pet drifts into a deep, comfortable sleep before the final goodbye
- The peaceful passing, while you hold them
- Time afterwards - I accommodate your pace
- Paw-print or fur-clipping keepsake on request
- Coordination with aftercare and cremation services - full service back to your home

## Quality-of-Life Consultation

Not sure if it's time? A separate, quieter visit to talk through how your pet is doing. I use practical, evidence-based scales (such as the HHHHHMM scale) so you can make a confident, informed decision - without pressure.

## Aftercare Coordination

Dr. Balsara works with established Lower Mainland providers for private and communal cremation. Aftercare wishes as per your instructions.
