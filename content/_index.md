## When the time comes, you don't have to drive anywhere

Saying goodbye to a beloved pet is one of the hardest things you may ever do. Dr. Balsara comes to your home so your dog or cat can rest in their favourite spot — on their own bed, in the sun, in your lap — without the stress of a clinic visit.

Visits are unhurried. There is time for questions, for goodbyes, and for the people who need to be there. See [what a visit includes](/services/), or [meet Dr. Balsara](/about/).

## What an in-home visit looks like

- A short conversation about how your pet has been doing
- A gentle sedative so your pet drifts into a deep, comfortable sleep
- The final injection, given peacefully while you hold them
- Time afterwards, for as long as you need
- Coordination with aftercare and cremation providers, if you'd like

Not sure if it's time? A separate [quality-of-life consultation](/services/) is available, and the [FAQ](/faq/) covers the questions families ask most often.

Dr. Balsara travels to families across the City of Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, the North Shore, Coquitlam, New Westminster, Port Moody, and surrounding communities. Browse [service areas](/service-areas/) or call — most of the Lower Mainland can be served.
