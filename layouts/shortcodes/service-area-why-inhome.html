Why families choose an in-home visit

{{- if eq $focus "car-ride" }}

Many older pets find car rides painful or stressful — and a longer drive can add one more hard moment on an already difficult day. An in-home visit removes that final journey and lets your dog or cat rest somewhere familiar, surrounded by the people who love them most.

{{- else }}

A clinic visit is hard on a pet who is already uncomfortable, and the trip itself can be stressful. An in-home visit means your dog or cat can rest in their favourite spot, with the people who love them most.
